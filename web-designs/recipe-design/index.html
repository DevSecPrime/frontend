Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk powder (khoya)
  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar syrup
  • Cardamom powder
  • Rose water

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder with flour and knead into soft dough with milk.
  2. Shape into small balls and deep fry until golden brown.
  3. Soak the fried balls in warm sugar syrup flavored with cardamom and rose water for 2 hours.

Barfi Penda

Ingredients

  • Condensed milk
  • Milk powder
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts

Recipe

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and add milk powder, stirring continuously.
  2. Add condensed milk and cook until mixture thickens and leaves the pan sides.
  3. Add cardamom, spread in greased tray, garnish with nuts, cool and cut into squares.
